Osu Basics


What is Osu!

Osu! is a free-to-play pc rhythm game where you move your cursor and tap circles to the beat.

For every good score you achive on a certain map, you are rewarded with PP or performance points that dictates your rank in the world.

Playing the game requires different skillsets, but the main 2 are streaming and aim.

Streaming

  1. Streaming is a consectutive hit of circles that flow in a pattern.
  2. Bursts are a pattern of circles that number less than 8 circles and once the number exceeds 8 then the pattern becomes a stream.
  3. Don't jump straight into high difficulty maps before mastering high 3 star and low 4 star maps.
  4. Start with maps that are in the 160-180 bpm range.

Aim

  1. There are two subcategories in aim. One being raw aim and the other being reading
  2. Raw aim is the ability to move one cursor from one place to another and a variety of features can affect your raw aim.
    • Sensitivity
    • Playing with a mouse or drawing tablet
    • Resolution
  3. There is also more sub categories of aim. Snappy and not snappy.
    • Snappy aim is ability to follow patterns with precision and accuracy.
    • Non snappy aim is the opposite of snappy aim where you are not as accurate in aiming and there is a more circular motion to your aiming.
  4. Reading is the ability to use your vision, pattern recognition, and game knowledge to interpret the rhythem and flow of a pattern.
    • To practice reading don't replay the same map over and over and consistently practice new and different maps.
